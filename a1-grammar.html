📖 Lesson 1 — The Verb "to be"

The verb to be is one of the most important verbs in English. We use it to talk about people, places and things.

🌿 Forms

Subject Verb
Iam
Youare
Heis
Sheis
Itis
Weare
Theyare

✨ Examples

✅ I am a student.

✅ She is my teacher.

✅ They are happy.

✅ We are friends.

❌ Negative

I am not tired.

He isn't busy.

They aren't here.

❓ Questions

Am I late?

Are you ready?

Is she happy?

⚠ Common Mistakes

❌ I is happy.

✅ I am happy.

❌ She are my friend.

✅ She is my friend.

❌ They is here.

✅ They are here.
