🌱 Chapter I — The Verb "to be"

The verb to be is one of the most important verbs in English. It is used to talk about people, places, feelings and things.

📖 Theory

am is used with I.

is is used with He, She, It.

are is used with You, We, They.

Subject Verb
I am
He / She / It is
You / We / They are

✨ Examples

  • I am a student.
  • She is my sister.
  • He is eleven years old.
  • We are friends.
  • They are at school.
  • It is cold today.

💡 Tips

✅ I am

✅ He is

✅ She is

✅ It is

✅ You are

✅ We are

✅ They are

⚠ Common Mistakes

  • ❌ I is happy.
  • ✅ I am happy.

  • ❌ She am tired.
  • ✅ She is tired.

  • ❌ They is late.
  • ✅ They are late.

📝 Summary

I → am

He / She / It → is

You / We / They → are
